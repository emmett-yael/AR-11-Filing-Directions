This flowchart is intended be a guide for those attempting to change their address for immigration purposes to find out where to send their paperwork.


The form to change an immigrant's address is called a Change of Address form and is also known as Form AR-11. The AR-11 must be filed with USCIS (United States Customs and Immigration Services) using certified mail


Changing your address via the United States Postal Service will *not* change your address with USCIS.

Your address must always be current with USCIS for immigration purposes.
